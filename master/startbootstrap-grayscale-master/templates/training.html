It is all about how she trains

Training needs to be sensitive to hormonal changes as the recovery and muscle growth is affeted by the hormonal levels

Climbers

Our hormonal balance affects all kinds of things from the energy systems we use, to how we build muscle (with more difficulty), and how we store body fat (with more ease). Since our bodies are designed for childbirth, a lot of the notable differences between men and women are related to that. For example, we carry more essential body fat than men (12% vs 4%) in order to support pregnancy. And we carry most of it on our lower body (more weight to pull up the wall on steep climbs!).
